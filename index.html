Sunflower

Naufal Arib Fadhlurrohman | Posted on 12 April 2023

Sunflower is a type of plant that is known for its large, bright yellow flowers that resemble the sun. The scientific name for sunflower is Helianthus annuus, which comes from the Greek words "helios" (sun) and "anthos" (flower).

Tulip

Naufal Arib Fadhlurrohman | Posted on 12 April 2023

Tulip is a genus of flowering plants that belongs to the Liliaceae family, which includes lilies, onions, and garlic. Tulips are known for their brightly colored cup-shaped flowers that have three petals and three sepals.

Bougainvillea

Bougainvillea is a genus of flowering plants native to South America, but widely cultivated as ornamental plants in many parts of the world. The flowers of bougainvillea are small, tubular, and typically white or yellow in color, but what makes them especially striking are the large, papery bracts (modified leaves) that surround the flowers.s

Lavender

Naufal Arib Fadhlurrohman | Posted on 12 April 2023

Lavender is a flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae. The plant is native to the Old World and is known for its fragrant, purple flowers, which are used in perfumes, soaps, and other fragrances. Lavender flowers are small and clustered on spikes, and they can be various shades of purple, blue, or pink.

Sakura

Naufal Arib Fadhlurrohman | Posted on 12 April 2023

Sakura is the Japanese word for cherry blossom, which refers to the flower of several species of cherry trees, most notably the Japanese cherry, Prunus serrulata. Sakura flowers are known for their delicate and beautiful appearance, with five petals that are typically pale pink or white in color.
